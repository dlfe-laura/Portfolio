Hi, I'm Laura! I'm originally from Spain, although I have done my higher education abroad, earning my Bachelor's degree in Physics from the University of Groningen in 2022. During my time in The Netherlands, I also pursued a minor in Gender and Diversity in Science, Society and Culture.

After my undergraduate studies I joined Top Dutch Solar Racing as an Electrical Engineer, contributing to innovative projects. In 2024 I decided to continue my studies, starting my Master's degree in Nanoscience at Lund University.

Aside from my educational and professional career, I advocate for LGBTQ+ rights and representation in STEM fields. One of my personal goals is to give voice and role models to the queer youth so science and engineering becomes more diverse.
