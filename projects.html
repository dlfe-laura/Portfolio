VOICes Engineering & Science

VOICes Engineering & Science is a project that was born from the need of representation in STEM fields. As a co-founder of the project, our aim was to give voice to LGBTQ+ online creators in fields of science and engineering. We strive for a more diverse future in academia and industry. The website allows anyone to search for LGBTQ+ creators from any scientific field in diverse social media platforms.

Top Dutch Solar Racing

Top Dutch Solar Racing (TDSR) is a Dutch racing team from which I was part in Team 2023. I took the role of Electrical Engineer in the team, being responsible of the design, development and building of the solar deck that would power the car for more than 3000 km through the Australian desert. Green Thunder, the 2023 TDSR car, would compete in the Bridgestone World Solar Challenge 2023 and end in 6th position. This car was the first in the world to compete using silicon-perovskite tandem solar cells, as the solar deck was developed in collaboration with the Oxford University's spin-off company, Oxford PV.
