VOICes Engineering & Science

VOICes Engineering & Science is a project that was born from the need of representation in STEM fields. As a co-founder of the project, our aim was to give voice to LGBTQ+ online creators in fields of science and engineering. We strive for a more diverse future in academia and industry. The website allows anyone to search for LGBTQ+ creators from any scientific field in diverse social media platforms.

Top Dutch Solar Racing

I was part of Top Dutch Solar Racing as an Electrical Engineer in their 2023 team. My responsabilities included the design, development, and manufacturing of the solar deck that powered the car for over 3000 km through the Australian desert. Our car, Green Thunder, competed in the Bridgestone World Solar Challenge 2023 and finished in 6th place. Notably, it was the first car in the world to compete using silicon-perovskite tandem solar cells. This pioneering solar deck was developed in collaboration with Oxford PV, the spin-off company from Oxford University.
